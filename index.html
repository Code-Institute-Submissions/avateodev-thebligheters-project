Who are we

We are "The Blighters" and we are London's most dominant and up and coming tag team that the professional wrestling has ever seen. Our roots go back since 1886 during the industrial era, with a legacy built over decades of running the streets of London as one of the most feared group of individuals.

Our Purpose

We are looking to dominate the tag-team division in the world of profesisonal wrestling. We want to conquer every mountain top and prove to the world that "The Blighters" will go down in history as on of the greatest professional wrestling tag-teams of all times. We do not care who will have to face. We do not see our opponents as challangers but merely stepping stones to greatness.
